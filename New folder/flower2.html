Red Roses

Origin Of Red Roses

Red roses, like other roses, are believed to have originated primarily in Asia. The cultivation and hybridization of roses have a long history, with evidence of rose cultivation dating back thousands of years in regions such as China, Persia (modern-day Iran), and the Mediterranean. The specific species of wild roses that contributed to the development of red roses are native to these regions. Over time, red roses have been bred and cultivated extensively around the world, leading to the many varieties we see today.
