Flower Constellation

Origin of Pink Roses:
Roses, in general, are believed to have originated in Asia, with fossil evidence suggesting they have been around for 35 million years.Specifically, many species of roses, including the ones that produce pink flowers, are native to Asia. Over time, roses have been cultivated and hybridized in various parts of the world.
Origin of Red Carnation:
Carnations (Dianthus caryophyllus) are native to the Mediterranean region. They have been cultivated for over 2,000 years, primarily in Europe, where they have become popular ornamental flowers.The red carnation, in particular, has become a symbol of love and admiration.
