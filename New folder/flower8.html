Origin of Pink Roses

Historical records suggest that the cultivation of roses began in Asia, particularly in China, several thousand years ago. From there, roses spread to other parts of the world through trade and exploration. Pink roses have been cherished for their beauty and fragrance for centuries and have become a staple in gardens and floral arrangements globally.

Origin of Red Roses

Roses are believed to have originated in Asia around 35 million years ago. They were first cultivated in ancient China and then spread to the Middle East and Europe through trade routes. By the time of the Roman Empire, roses were widely grown and appreciated across Europe.

Origin of Lavender Roses

Lavender-colored roses are a relatively recent development in the history of rose cultivation, primarily created through hybridization efforts in the late 20th century. These roses are the result of crossbreeding between various rose species to achieve the unique lavender hue. Their origin can be traced back to modern rose breeding programs in Europe and the United States.
